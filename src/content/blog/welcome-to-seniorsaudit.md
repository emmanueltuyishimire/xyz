## Our Founding Goal

Welcome to the Seniors Audit educational blog! 

We launched this website because we believe that retirees, seniors, and caregivers shouldn't face planning anxiety simply because government rules are written in complicated legal language.

Our mission is simple: **We translate complex government rules and calculations into clear, plain English.**

## What You Will Find Here

We publish two main types of resources:

1. **Interactive Calculators:** Confidential tools built from official tax codes and formulas (such as Social Security Full Retirement Age charts or Medicare IRMAA income brackets) that let you calculate estimates in seconds.
2. **Plain-English Guides:** Step-by-step checklists, sample letters, and simple definitions that explain how benefit programs work without the legal jargon.

## Our Core Commitments

* **No Sales Pitch:** We are 100% educational. We do not sell insurance, investment portfolios, or legal services.
* **Senior-Friendly Access:** Our site is built with large text, high contrast, and clean layouts, ensuring readability.
* **Accuracy & Trust:** Every guide we write is based directly on official government documents (like IRS publications or Social Security manuals).

We are glad you are here, and we hope our resources make your retirement planning easier!
